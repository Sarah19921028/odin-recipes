Glazed Donut

This is a simple glazed donut recipe that is easy to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk (for glaze)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients until just combined.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375
  5. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil, frying until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Remove donuts and drain on paper towels.
  7. In a small bowl, mix powdered sugar and 2 tablespoons milk to make a glaze.
  8. Dip the warm donuts into the glaze, coating them evenly.
  9. Let the glazed donuts cool on a wire rack.

Enjoy your glazed donuts!
