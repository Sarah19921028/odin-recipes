Fudge

This is a simple fudge recipe that is easy to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and butter.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture comes to a boil.
  3. Continue boiling for 4-5 minutes without stirring, until it reaches the soft ball stage (234°F or 112°C).
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and nuts if using.
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased 8-inch square pan.
  6. Let it cool at room temperature until set, about 2 hours.
  7. Once set, cut into squares and serve.

Enjoy your fudge!
